Did You Know? 🌟

  • 🎭 Sankranthi marks the start of the harvest season and is celebrated across India with unique traditions.
  • 🌞 It signifies the Sun's transition into Capricorn (Makara), a symbol of new beginnings.
  • 🌾 Traditional sweets like Ariselu and Pongal are prepared to honor the festival.
  • 🪁 Kite flying during Sankranthi is believed to strengthen bonds among communities.
